RICA or the Regulation of Interception of Communication Act is a law that has been passed by the South African government that makes it compulsory for prepaid and contract customers to register all new and existing cellphone numbers. Step 1: Go to www.turnmeon.co.za



Step 2: Click on REGISTER SIM CARD



Step 3: Select whether you are a SA Citizen, Foreign National living in SA or a Temporary Visitor.



Step 4: Upload your RICA Documents and click on Next.

Ensure you have the following RICA Documents:
• South African ID / Foreign Passport
• A selfie holding your identification
• Proof of Address (not required if you are a tourist)



Step 5: Enter your South African ID Number or your Foreign Passport Number and click Next.



Step 6: Enter your First Name, Surname, Alternative Contact Number and Email Address and click Next.



Step 7: Enter your Address Details and click Next.



Step 8: Once your personal details have been verified, click Next.



Step 9: Select I HAVE A SIM CARD and enter the ICCID Number and click on Next.
The ICCID Number is the 20-digit number either on the front or the back of the SIM Card.



Step 10: Once the SIM has been RICA'd successfully, insert the SIM Card into a phone and wait for the SMS with your new number.
